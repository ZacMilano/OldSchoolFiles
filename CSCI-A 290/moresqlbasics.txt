- Deletion rules
  - Restrict deletion : the requested record cannot be deleted until all related
    records in any "subordinate" (one <-> one) tables or in the "many" side of a
    (one <-> many) tables are deleted
  - Cascade deletion : deleting the requested record automatically deletes any
    records in the "subordinate" or "many" tables
    - TEN CUIDADO
- Standards
  - ANSI, ISO
- SELECT, FROM : not optional
- WHERE, GROUP BY, HAVING : optional
